California College San Diego

California College San Diego is dedicated to helping students graduate and get a better job sooner. We focus on educating students for rewarding careers and enriching lives. Our goal is for students to achieve success in career-oriented programs and in the enhancement of skills in critical thinking, communication, problem solving, and technology, culminating in rewarding career placement or advancement in current employment.
