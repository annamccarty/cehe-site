Careers at CEHE

Grow your career and gain your independence with us

Whether you’re interested in admissions, academics, financial aid, or career services, CEHE employees’ goals are one and the same: to foster success for all our graduates. We believe that helping our students achieve and grow in their careers means helping our employees grow in theirs. Discover the benefits of working for Center for Excellence in Higher Education.

Your Next Move

If you believe that helping others achieve their dreams is part of your life’s mission, then our colleges offer a wide variety of employment opportunities for your personal and professional growth.

We’re looking for intelligent, driven, productive, talented, and innovative faculty and staff members to help our students succeed. Join our family by applying today.

Taking Care of Our Team

At Center for Excellence in Higher Education, you work hard for our students. Shouldn’t we work hard for you? CEHE is constantly striving to provide top-notch benefits and rewards for its employees. We offer a wide array of benefits and competitive compensation for employees, as well as their families. Learn more about working for Center for Excellence in Higher Education by applying today.

A Full Range of Benefits

  • Medical and dental insurance
  • Vision care
  • HSA and FSA accounts
  • Employee assistance programs
  • Short-term & long-term disability
  • Vacation/sick/holiday pay
  • Retirement plan
  • Education benefits (earn your degree at one of our colleges)
