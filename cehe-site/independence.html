Independence
University

Independence University's flexible, fully online degree programs are strictly career focused. We’ve eliminated unnecessary elective courses, so students can graduate and get started in a new career as fast as possible. In addition, IU courses are designed using input and advice from our nationwide network of business partners to help ensure the knowledge and skills students learn match what today’s employers are looking for.

“Our mission as a non-profit is to provide the highest quality, career-focused education that we can and to do everything to take students, give them the education they need in a specific career, work with them as far as employment assistance and career services, have them employed, and really see their life change. That’s what we’re all about.”

—Eric Juhlin
President of Independence University
